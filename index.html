The Science

HOW IT
WORKS

1
Dip the Pen

Discreetly dip the tip of SafeSips into your drink for just 2 seconds. That's it.

2
Microfluidic Detection

Three tiny channels inside pull a micro-sample through the pen, each one designed to detect a different type of drug.

3
Color Reaction

If a drug is present, a reaction produces a specific color. An optical sensor reads the result instantly.

4
Instant Alert

Your phone gets a notification in under 5 seconds — and your emergency contacts are ready to be alerted.
