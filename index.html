The Science, Simplified

HOW IT
WORKS

1
Dip the Pen

Discreetly dip the tip of SafeSips into your drink for just 2 seconds. No one around you will even notice.

2
Micro-Sample Flows In

Three tiny channels inside pull a micro-sample of your drink through the pen — no pumps needed, just natural capillary action.

3
Color Reaction Happens

If a drug is present, a targeted chemical reaction produces a specific color. A tiny optical sensor reads the result instantly.

4
Instant Alert

Your phone vibrates in under 5 seconds. Your pre-chosen emergency contacts are ready to be alerted — all automatically, all discreetly.
